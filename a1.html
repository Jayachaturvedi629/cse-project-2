Nigeria

Nigeria is known for its diverse cultures and rich traditions. The country is home to over 250 ethnic groups, each with its unique customs and attire.

Kenya

Kenya's culture is a blend of various ethnic groups, with the Maasai being one of the most recognized for their vibrant attire and beadwork.

South Africa

South Africa boasts a rich cultural heritage, with influences from indigenous tribes, colonial history, and modern urban life. Traditional attire varies widely across different regions.

Egypt

Egyptian culture is one of the oldest in the world, known for its ancient history, art, and traditional clothing that reflects its rich heritage.
