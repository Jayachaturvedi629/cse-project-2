Spain

Spain boasts a vibrant cultural heritage with regional variations. The flamenco dress is a famous symbol of Andalusia, known for its ruffles, bright colors, and intricate details.

Scotland

Scotland's traditional attire includes the kilt, a pleated tartan garment worn by men. Tartans represent different clans and families, showcasing heritage and pride.

Netherlands

Traditional Dutch clothing varies by region, but some recognizable items include clogs (klompen) - wooden shoes - and for women, the kap dracht, a traditional headwear with regional variations.
